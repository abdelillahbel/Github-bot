Welcome to WiniWalk

WiniWalk is a motivating walking app designed to turn every step into a chance to win exciting rewards and prizes. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or just looking to stay active, WiniWalk provides a unique and enjoyable way to track your walking progress.

App Features

  • Step Tracking
  • Challenges and Rewards
  • Referral Program
  • Walk History
  • Community and Competition
  • Personalized Goals
  • Firebase Integration
